Description

Cannabis Meets Aluminum

This engraved grinder is four pieces of anodized aluminum glory.

It’s made with ultra-durable material, making it withstand your daily wear-and-tear.

This aluminum model gets a crisp, clean grind every time, thanks to its super sharp teeth.

The Grinder Made for Merchandise

The no-frills design of the 4-piece aluminum grinder makes it the perfect blank slate for your brand image.

With ten colors to choose from and our state-of-the-art laser engraving technology, the possibilities are endless.

This grinder is the perfect option for revamping your brand image, while bringing your customers premium grinder quality you can trust.

Grinders Made Your Way

With a focus on quality, innovation, and design, MunchMakers has become the go-to manufacturer for unique and durable grinders.

Our advanced customization options allow brands to showcase their identity on products their customers will love.

From precision laser engraving to full-color printing, we ensure every detail reflects excellence. Whether you need grinders for retail, promotional events, or subscription boxes, MunchMakers delivers products that stand out and elevate your brand.
